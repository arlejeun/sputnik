Inboud Sales Dashboard

\n\n

\n \n \n \n

\n The Sales Supervisor Dashboard offers contact center managers and supervisors real-time access to the operational KPIs to monitor the status of your queues and gain actionable insights into your team performance.\n

\n\n

What you'll learn

\n\n
    \n
  • Visualize trends of service level across your queues and volume of customer calls with your organization today
    • \n
  • Identify at a glance and in real-time the distribution of your teams by status
    • \n
  • Measure the service level as well as other key performance indicators to measure the performance of your routing distribution
    • \n
\n\n

What you'll need

\n

\n
    \n
  • Genesys CIM 8.1+
    • \n
  • Genesys Pulse 8.5.106+
    • \n
